Profile

Algorithmically rigorous. Systems-minded. Comfortable shipping ideas.

My research is driven by a simple question: how do we make hard graph mining problems practical at modern scale without giving up exactness?

Right now I focus on GPU-accelerated algorithms for dense subgraph discovery, quasi-clique mining, and related clique-relaxation problems where both pruning strategy and hardware behavior matter.

Before research became my main lane, I worked in cloud engineering at Deloitte USI. Building integrations and pipelines across Oracle, Workday, Salesforce, Stripe, and other production systems taught me to think beyond prototypes. I still carry that bias: elegant ideas should survive contact with real workloads.
